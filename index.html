Hello!

My name is Juhi Kedia and I’m from Jaipur, India. I have been obsessed with creativity ever since I remember. For me, Art always speaks where words are unable to express. I see design in every aspect be it a building’s perspective, cuts in dresses, the flexibility in furniture, the body of an automobile, or even a cup I drink coffee from. Along-with my love for art and design, I am equally passionate for nature, dance, sports and academics. I would like to pursue a field of study which helps me enhance my knowledge and sharpen my perspective for a purposeful life. I am interested in pursuing either the P or the E track.
